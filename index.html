About Wampas

Wampas helps you build a better discord server, be it for you and your friends, or for an organisation or event. with a plethora of features, ranging from moderation, to music, to your very own google engine, Wampas is built to compliment the needs of every discord server out there.

With Wampas, getting lyrics was never easier! You can search lyrics for any and every song out there! and here's the catch, you don't have to be listening to it ;). Along with it's memes, roasts and inspiration features, Wampas has got something very special for all the marvel fans out there! Use the earth616 command to find out which marvel character you are!

The small talk feature on Wampas let's you converse with the bot! Use Wampas hi to initiate the chat session and Wampas bye to end it. You can ask Wampas anything you like! try: "Hey, How you doin?"
